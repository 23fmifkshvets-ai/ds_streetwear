DS Core Tee

The DS Core Tee is the heart of DS STREETWEAR. Minimalistic, powerful, and uncompromisingly modern. A shirt for people who don’t need loud prints to show presence.

The design intentionally stays clean and timeless: on the front, only the small DS-Crest logo, placed subtly and elegantly. No unnecessary elements — just style, quality, and attitude.

The cut is modern oversized with extra-long sleeves reaching the elbows, giving the shirt a strong streetwear look. The fabric is extremely soft, comfortable on the skin, and high-quality, making it a piece you’ll want to wear every day.

Available in 240 g and 300 g fabric weight — depending on whether you prefer a lighter feel or an especially heavy premium feel.
