About DS STREETWEAR

DS STREETWEAR is an independent streetwear brand founded on December 1, 2025 in Bad Salzungen, Thuringia, Germany.

The brand was created with a clear idea: to bring gothic streetwear and minimalist design to a new level.

Many streetwear pieces today are either too simple or overloaded with unnecessary graphics. DS STREETWEAR combines both worlds — a dark, gothic aesthetic with clean, modern minimalism.

Our designs are inspired by gothic elements, symbolism, knights, dark art, and modern streetwear culture. At the same time, many pieces remain intentionally minimalistic, making them timeless, powerful, and easy to style.

A strong focus of the brand is premium quality and oversized fits. Our shirts are made from heavy, high-quality fabrics (such as 240g and 300g cotton) that provide durability while feeling soft and comfortable.

DS STREETWEAR is made for people who want their clothing to express their identity — on the street, in everyday life, or in the gym.

Currently, DS STREETWEAR is available exclusively online, but the vision continues to grow.

DS STREETWEAR represents a new generation of streetwear: dark, minimal, and uncompromising in quality.
